# Audit Rubrics

## Landing Page Rubric

Score each area from 1 to 5.

### Clarity

- Is the offer understandable within a few seconds?
- Is the category or use case clear?

### Relevance

- Does it speak to a real buyer pain?
- Does it match the likely audience?

### Differentiation

- Does it say why this product is different?
- Is the comparison against alternatives visible?

### Proof

- Are there results, examples, customer evidence, or concrete claims?

### Hierarchy

- Is the most important claim the easiest to notice?
- Do sections follow a sensible buying logic?

### CTA Strength

- Is the action specific?
- Does the CTA text reflect value?

## Pricing Page Rubric

- Is the pricing model easy to understand?
- Are plan differences meaningful?
- Are objections handled?
- Is the buyer guided toward the right plan?
- Are there proof and trust elements near the decision point?

## SEO Content Rubric

- Does the page match clear search intent?
- Is the angle differentiated enough to compete?
- Does the content teach, compare, or convert with purpose?
- Are headings useful and specific?
- Is there a next step for the reader?

## Competitor Messaging Rubric

- What promise do competitors lead with?
- What proof do they use?
- What audience are they clearly targeting?
- What objections do they answer better?
- Where do they all sound the same?

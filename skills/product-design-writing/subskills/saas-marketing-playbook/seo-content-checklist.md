# SEO Content Checklist

## Intent

- What is the likely search intent?
- Is this informational, comparative, or transactional?
- Does the page actually satisfy that intent?

## Topic and Angle

- Is the topic worth targeting for the business?
- Is the angle differentiated from generic search results?
- Does the page reflect a real point of view or original experience?

## On-Page Structure

- Strong title with a clear promise
- Useful H1
- Specific H2s and H3s
- Scannable structure
- Clear internal next steps

## Conversion Relevance

- Is there a logical path from search traffic to product value?
- Does the page connect the topic to the product naturally?
- Is the CTA aligned with reader intent and stage?

## Quality Warnings

Watch for:

- content written only to rank, with no business value
- interchangeable intros and generic definitions
- vague claims with no examples or proof
- no opinion, no experience, and no differentiation
- strong traffic potential but weak conversion intent
